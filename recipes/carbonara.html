Carbonara

Description

Here is my recipe for carbonara. It is super simple and made from Ingredients I often have in the fridge already. (1 Serving)

Nutritional Information

(All values estimated)

  • Protein: 38g
  • Carbohydrates: 52g
  • Fat: 31g
  • Total Calories: 526kcal

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti - 70g
  • Egg (Medium) - 1
  • Grated Cheddar/Parmesan - 40g
  • Oil - 1 tsp
  • Bacon/Pancetta - 50g lardons or 2 rashers
  • Garlic - 2 cloves
  • Salt - to taste
  • Pepper - to taste
  • Parsley - garnish

Method

  1. Boil the spaghetti for 10 minutes in lightly salted water.
  2. In a bowl, crack the egg and mix with the grated cheese.
  3. Heat the oil in a frying pan and add the bacon lardons (or cut rashers into small strips and add). Fry until slightly browned.
  4. Fry the garlic with the bacon until golden.
  5. Add the cooked spaghetti to the frying pan and stir until fully mixed.
  6. Take the frying pan off the heat and let it cool for 1 minute.
  7. Add the egg and cheese mixture to the spaghetti and stir continuously until the cheese has fully melted.
  8. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
  9. Optionally garnish with parsley, and enjoy!

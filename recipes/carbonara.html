Carbonara

Description

Here is my recipe for carbonara. It is super simple and made from Ingredients I often have in the fridge already.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Cheese
  • 1 x Egg
  • Bacon
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Method

  1. Boil the spaghetti.
  2. Cut the bacon into small strips and fry.
  3. In a bowl, mix the egg and cheese.
  4. Add the garlic to the bacon in the frying pan.
  5. Add the cooked spaghetti to the frying pan and stir until fully mixed.
  6. Take the frying pan off the heat and let cool for 1 minute.
  7. Add the egg and cheese mixture to the spaghetti and stir continuously until the cheese has fully melted.
  8. Season to taste with salt and pepper.

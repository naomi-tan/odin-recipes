Sausage and Mash

Description

Here is my recipe for sausage and mash (or bangers and mash). This is the perfect comfort food.

Ingredients

  • Sausages
  • Potatoes
  • Cheese
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Peas
  • Onion
  • Gravy granules
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Method

  1. Put the sausages in the oven.
  2. Peel and dice the potatoes and boil in a saucepan.
  3. Drain the potatoes when they fall off fork.
  4. Mash the potatoes.
  5. Add butter to the potatoes while mashing.
  6. Add the cheese to the potatoes and mix in. They may need to be put on very low heat to melt the cheese.
  7. Add a dash of milk and whip to make fluffy.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Boil peas.
  10. Fry onions until brown and slightly caramelised.
  11. Add gravy granules and water to the onions and stir until thick.

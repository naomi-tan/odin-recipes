Chilli Con Carne

Description

Here is my recipe for chilli con carne. I have found that adding brown sugar gives a much more enhanced flavour.

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • 1 x White onion
  • 500g Minced beef or alternative
  • Paprika
  • Ground coriander
  • Cumin
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Oregano
  • Garlic powder
  • 1 x Beef stock cube
  • 4 x Cloves garlic
  • 1 x Tin chopped tomatoes
  • 1 x Tin red kidney beans
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Brown sugar
  • Cheese
  • Soured cream or creme fraiche
  • Boiled rice

Method

  1. Add oil to a pan and heat up on medium heat.
  2. Add onions and cook until soft.
  3. Add mince or alternative and cook.
  4. Add garlic and cook.
  5. Add parpika, ground coriander, cumin, cayenne pepper and beef stock.
  6. Add tinned tomatoes.
  7. Add oregano and garlic powder.
  8. Add red kidney beans.
  9. Season to taste with salt, pepper and brown sugar.
  10. Serve with grated cheese and soured cream or creme fraiche on boiled rice.
